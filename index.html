Welcome to the Playbook

This interactive guide is designed to replace static documents and provide a single source of truth for managing job family changes. Our goal is to empower you with a clear, step-by-step process, practical tools, and key insights to ensure a smooth and successful transition for everyone involved.

Key Themes & Principles

Three core principles guide every successful job family change.

Fairness & Equity

Ensure all decisions are objective, data-driven, and consistently applied to build trust and maintain morale.

Key Action: Conduct an equity review before finalizing any changes.
  • Standardized evaluation criteria
  • Unbiased calibration sessions
  • Clear appeals process

Manager Enablement

Equip people managers with the tools, training, and talking points they need to lead their teams through the change.

Key Action: Hold dedicated training sessions for all impacted managers.
  • Manager-specific FAQ docs
  • Role-playing difficult conversations
  • Direct line to HR support

Transparent Communication

Proactively share information about the process, timeline, and rationale to reduce anxiety and speculation.

Key Action: Create a central communication hub for all updates.
  • Regular progress updates
  • Clear "What's Next" sections
  • Opportunities for Q&A

The 5-Phase Process

A structured approach from initial idea to final implementation.

Objectives & Activities

Define the problem, scope the project, align with stakeholders, and conduct initial data analysis. Use the AI Charter Builder for a head start.

Engage senior leadership early to secure sponsorship.

Objectives & Activities

Develop the new job family structure, career paths, competency models, and write job descriptions. The AI Job Description Generator is a powerful tool here.

Co-create with business leaders, don't design in a silo.

Objectives & Activities

Pressure-test the new design with managers, run calibration sessions, and analyze the financial impact. Be open to feedback and ready to iterate.

Model the compensation impact carefully.

Objectives & Activities

Execute the change, communicate to all employees, update HR systems, and manage the transition. Use the Communication Plan template to stay organized.

Manager readiness is the #1 predictor of success.

Objectives & Activities

Monitor the impact of the change, conduct post-launch surveys, and embed it in ongoing talent processes. Schedule a 6-month review to assess long-term success.

The work isn't over on launch day.
